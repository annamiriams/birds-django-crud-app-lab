About

BirdBase was created to help bird lovers and curious observers keep track of the birds visiting their backyards. Log sightings, record what foods the birds are eating, and reflect on seasonal changes or feeding habits. Whether you're just starting out or have years of birdwatching behind you, this tool is here to make your observations meaningful and easy to revisit.
